Myrrh

Mission

To nurture a Christ-centered home that cultivates a love for learning, a strong foundation in scripture, and lifelong discipleship in our children for the glory of God in Christ and the good of His church.

Vision

To cultivate a generation deeply rooted in God's word, and passionate about serving Him and others. We aim to instill habits of mind and heart that enable the students to a lifelong pursuit of truth, grace, wisdom, and excellence, fostering a commitment to make Christ known in all of life.

Core Values

Faith: We believe in the Lordship of Christ, and the authority of scripture and strive to integrate biblical principles into all aspects of our homeschooling journey for the praise of God's glorious grace.

Excellence: We are committed to providing a high-quality education that equips our children with the biblical view of knowledge and God-given skills necessary to thrive.

Discipleship: We aim to foster a love for God and the church in our children and encourage them to grow in their faith and dependence on God.

Love: We create a loving and supportive learning environment where our children, made in the image of God, feel encouraged to explore, ask questions, and make mistakes.

Family: We prioritize God's word in our family and believe that learning should be a collaborative effort between parents and children as we depend on God's grace.

Service: We instill the importance of selflessly serving others and encourage our children to use their gifts and talents to benefit their communities.
